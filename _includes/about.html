I currently build code at Goldman Sachs and live in Mumbai, India. I studied computer science at BITS Pilani and pursued my undergraduate thesis with Andrew Lippman at the MIT Media Lab.


I like to work on projects that are design-oriented and solve real-world human-computer interaction (HCI) problems. I like to work with people who value empathy-driven development. My research interests are interdisciplinary - I enjoy blending ideas from HCI and product design with literature, cinema, and economics. During my time at MIT, I worked on politics and computer vision for a grassroots-level analysis of news media. Here is an interview on my experience!

I soon coded up this website and helped implement one of my favourite tabletop games. At university, I was also a student representative for Microsoft Codess.
