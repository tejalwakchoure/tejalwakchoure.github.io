Visualized the layout of The Banner's homepage on the night of the General Election and integrated AP widgets to fit the style language of the rest of the site.

Built a voter guide for the 2024 Maryland Primary and the General Election with its own contained designs. Both the guides got high page views and furthered our efforts in utility journalism. I also got the chance to present this work at NICAR!

The voter guide implemented several new components fed via our CMS, like improved navigation and calls to action, an accordion questionnaire for profiles and a live blog for election day.

H/T to THE CITY's Meet Your Mayor and the LAist's Voter Game Plan.
